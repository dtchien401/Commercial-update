New Project Phase 2

Commercial Support Execution Arm

Designed to close the gap between Head Office strategy and club-floor execution.

May 2026 Priority

Convert

Guest to Member

Improve conversion through consistent sales flow, correct pricing and clear product explanation.

Optimize

Member to Ecosystem

Increase lifetime value by connecting members to PT, Supplements, Hypoxi and Vita.

Retain

Usage to Loyalty

Drive usage, results, proactive care and renewal readiness.

Problem to Solve

  • Inconsistent execution: Club results depend too much on individual manager capability.
  • Pricing noise: Unauthorized benefit splitting creates messy quotation and discount pressure.
  • Low trust: Customers and field teams lose confidence when offer logic is not consistent.

Expected Outcome

  • Better conversion: Same product story and pricing discipline across clubs.
  • Higher PT penetration: Better handover from membership to PT ecosystem.
  • Lower discount leakage: Stronger control on offer, package and SKU logic.

Control Mechanism

  • Single SKU rule: Embedded benefits cannot be split and sold separately.
  • Audit: Random checks on online quotation, club posting and customer offer.
  • Enforcement: First offense = 50% income cut. Second offense = termination.
  • Manager accountability: 5,000,000 VND penalty if manager fails to control team compliance.
